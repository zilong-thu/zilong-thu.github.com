躲雨的猫

To be best viewed in webkit browsers such as Chrome and Safari. Firefox doesn't support CSS3 box-reflect property, so no reflection of the cat or the umbrella would be seen in FF. The following techs are used in this simple animation page:

  • CSS3 @keyframes, animation
  • CSS2 positioning.
  • Adobe Illustrator. To make the rain drop image.
